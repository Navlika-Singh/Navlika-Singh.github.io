About Me

I am an MSc student in Computing at Imperial College London, where I work with the iBUG Lab under the guidance of Dr. Antoni Bigata and Dr. Stavros Petridis. My current research focuses on the personalization of Lip-Sync, and by extension Talking Head, models, with broader interests in Generative AI, Vision–Language Modeling, and Alignment for Responsible AI systems.

Prior to my graduate studies, I completed my B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science with distinction(Top 10) at IIT Jodhpur, where I worked with Prof. Mayank Vatsa and Prof. Richa Singh on developing a novel Bias Mitigation framework. During my penultimate year, I was awarded the MITACS Globalink Research Internship, consequently, I worked with Prof. Christopher Whidden at Dalhousie University, Canada.

I am passionate about a lot of things outside of academics. I am intrigued by the studies of human conciousness, love reading books, and exploring new places. I am also a foodie.

If you like my work or would like to connect, feel free to reach out. I’m always happy to collaborate or just have a conversation .
