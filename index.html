About Me

I am an MSc student in Computing (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) at Imperial College London, where I aim to explore research directions at the intersection of Generative AI, Vision–Language Modeling, and Alignment for Responsible AI systems.

Prior to my graduate studies, I completed my Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at IIT Jodhpur, where I worked with Prof. Mayank Vatsa and Prof. Richa Singh on developing a novel bias mitigation framework to reduce neural networks' reliance on spurious correlations in data. During my penultimate year, I was also awarded the Mitacs Globalink Research Internship at Dalhousie University, Canada, where I worked on dataset selection and generalization for underwater fish detection.
